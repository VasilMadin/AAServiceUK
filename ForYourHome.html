We Are Award Winning Domestic Appliance Repair Company

Professional SERVICES for you and your family whenever you need them most...

Awards

Based in London , AA Service UK repair and service most makes of domestic appliances, specialising in integrated and freestanding appliances.

We provide repairs for Washing Machines, Washer Dryers, Tumble Dryers, Dishwashers, Fridges & Freezers, American Style Fridge Freezers, Double Door Fridge Freezers, Ovens, Vacuum Cleaners, and many other household appliances.

We offer a same/next day call out service we can provide you with an estimate on the cost of the repair.

When you invite AA Service UK into your home, you will encounter not only expertly trained technicians, but experts who are trained to deliver to you a tailored and unique service experience.

This experience includes treating you and your home with respect. Many of our customers look to AA Appliance Repair as their preferred service provider. We enjoy our work and it shows!

We look forward to welcoming you as a customer and being your preferred source for appliance service in the future. Some of the Residential Services

We are delighted to announce that AA Service UK have been awarded the Highly Commended award for Family run Appliance Repair Company of the Year at the Jemison Hotel London.
In presenting the award the judging panel commended the company for "Not only does the company demonstrate considerable determination and hard work, its approach to providing high levels of customer services throughout a wide variation of work were the main factors in it being awarded the Highly Commended accolade."
“To have a successful business it is paramount to have reliable members of staff that you can truly depend on and trust. We work hard to provide our customers with an excellent service and as a relatively small team we are driven to go the extra mile to please them,” explained our director.
“Our customers are at the heart of everything we do and it is essential that they are satisfied. We understand that if we provide a great service for a reasonable price, they will recommend us and this has been a key reason for our growth.”
